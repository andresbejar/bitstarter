Groopy is a social network entirely focused around digitalizing and reliving the experience of music concerts and shows. It allows users to follow their favorite bands, track tour dates and upcoming concerts. Then buy tickets to a concert. Buying tickets earns you rewards in the form of Trinkets and Badges, exclusively designed for the band. Checking in at the concert can earn you further rewards. All these rewards can then be placed in a profile we have called The Road, where you record the concerts you have gone to, the Trinkets you have earned and the path music has taken you. You can show who is the ultimate Groopy.

Users can also check which concerts and festivals their friends have gone to, compare trinkets and get recommendations on shows, bands and albums. Groopy will be the ultimate destination for all concert-related information. Groopy is not designed to be a music player, but rather a discovery tool for new music, new shows and a biographical tool, where users can immortalize their concert experiences.

FAQ

Can I buy tickets through Groopy?
Yes you can. With groopy you will be able to choose between several ticket providers and buy it at the best price. You will also automatically receive any rewards linked with the show.
What if I already bought the ticket through another source?
We are working on partnerships where you can still redeem the rewards using the ticket confirmation number or QR code.
